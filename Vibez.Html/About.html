Our first collection

We will always remember our very first collection, it played a big part in getting us where we are today. We kicked off in a garage, using a basic sublimation printer worth $150 and a heat press that cost us $200. One day, we came up with a design that caught a lot of attention. It was so good that a clothing festival organizer noticed it. He invited us to show our prototypes, and that's when we made our first sales.

Our journey

Vibez was just a dream that began with a spark of creativity. No matter the limitations we had, we immersed ourselves in the heart of urban culture. We listened to what people wanted and the changes that had to be made in the industry, with a sketch book, our vission and passion, we filled everyone hearts with our creativity. Our unique style made word spread fast, making us grow. What use to be a dream grew in to a local pop up shot, to a trend and online waves. Vibez became more than just clothing - it became a statement.

Our goal

Vibez goal is to empower individuals to showcase them selves as they are. We want you to embrace your authentic selve through urban fashion. Through our clothe we want you to feel that a dream can become more then that. We want to inspire people to follow their dreams, as we did.
- Vibez
